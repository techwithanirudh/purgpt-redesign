Chat completions are the models that are used in **text conversations** between user and AI. These models are, for example, `gpt-3.5-turbo`, `claude-instant-1`, ... [Learn more on the difference between chat completions and completions.](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/gpt/chat-completions-vs-completions)

PurGPT offers a variety of such models, created by OpenAI, Anthropic and Llama.
