# What are Completions?

The biggest question people ask about completions is: 

> What's the difference between `chat completions` and `completions`?

The answer to that question is quite simple: __Chat completions are specifically designed for conversational interactions, while completions refer to the general output of a language model.__

Chat completion models are focused on conversation, whereas completion refers to the overall ability of a model to generate coherent text.
